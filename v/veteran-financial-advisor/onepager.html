Key Findings

01 Veterans solve the retention crisis. Trained tolerance for sustained discomfort combats the #1 washout cause.
02 Interview weaknesses are fixable. Jargon, translation gap, nervousness — behavioral, not competency problems.
03 Firm programs exist but lack proof. Edward Jones, NW Mutual, First Command all have pipelines — none publish comparative data.
04 American College = key institution. Full scholarships for CFP®, ChFC®, CLU® + mentorship + placement.
05 SkillBridge is underutilized. 70%+ interest but only 32% participation. 58% of installations lack partnerships.

First-Year Risks

  • ▸ Prospecting Wall — 75% of rookies fail within 3 years from inability to build a client base
  • ▸ Income Shock — Commission-based pay vs. steady military salary + benefits
  • ▸ Role Misalignment — Expecting planning, spending 80% of time prospecting
  • ▸ Isolation — Military is communal; solo-advisor models feel isolating

What We Don’t Know

No published study compares veteran vs. non-veteran advisor performance on any metric. No veteran-specific FINRA pass rates. Some firm programs may be partly performative marketing. This is the most valuable study that could be conducted.
